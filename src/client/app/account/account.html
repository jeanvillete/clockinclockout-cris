Deleting account

We hope you don't wish to go!

But in case, whether you're certain you want to leave us, this is the process;

  1. Click the button bellow, then 'Delete account' and submit the request.
  2. An email is going to arrieve on your inbox, in order to ensure you really want to proceed.
  3. In order to ensure you are de facto the owner of the account, we ask you to check your inbox out and click the 'confirmation link', this way your account and all related data is going to be completely deleted.
